Welcome to

Monson Swimming Club

We are a friendly competitive swimming club based in Kent. We cater for all abilities from swimming for fitness, to competitive swimming. Our swimmers compete regularly in county, regional and national competitions, and frequently rank well nationally. We train at Monson Leisure Center and Valance School pool. We also have a Masters section, which caters for ex competitive swimmers or swimmers who used to swim regularly and want to get back into it. Masters also offers the options of competitive swimming and swimming for fitness.
